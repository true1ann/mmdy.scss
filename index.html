Q: How to change default colors?
A: Using the Official Material Theme Builder on the Web made by Google.
  1. Enter the Website
  2. Change colors
  3. Click on 'Pick your fonts'
  4. Click on 'Export theme'
  5. Click on 'Export'
  6. Click on 'Web (CSS)'
  7. Confirm downloading
  8. Open the downloaded archive
  9. Embed any of the .css files onto your website
  10. Thats it! Enjoy your new palette.
Tip: Just dont forget do rename whatever the class is to just :root, or else it wont work!
